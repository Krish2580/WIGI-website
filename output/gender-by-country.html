    Gender by country

    This plot shows the percentage of biographies of women by country of birth. Country is determined by seeing if the place of birth is a country, or if it is a city, see if the city has a country property in Wikidata.

    The changes show the change in total biographies, and change in percentage since the previous snapshot date.

    As of October 2014, only about 24% of biographies, had place of birth, so this data is incomplete.
