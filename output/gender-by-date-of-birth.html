    Gender by date of birth and death

    This plot shows the Date of Birth (DoB) and Date of Death (DoD) of each biography in Wikidata, by gender. Non-binary genders, by last count there are 9 nonbinary genders, are displayed in the tables, and accounted for in the full data set

    The changes show the change in totals since last snapshot, for each year.

    As of October 2014, only about 58% and 28% of biographies, had date of birth and date of death data, respectively, so this data is incomplete.
