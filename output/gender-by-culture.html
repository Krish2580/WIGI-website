    Gender by culture

    This plot shows the number of gendered biographies of men and women in Wikidata, by "Culture".

    culture is determined by using translating ethnic group, place of birth, and citizenship into 1 of 9 world cultures as per Inglehart-Welzel map of the world with Mechanical Turk. Then we take the consensus of the three aggregated variables. All aggregation maps are available for inspection on github.

    As of October 2014, only about 45% of biographies, had culture, so this data is incomplete.
